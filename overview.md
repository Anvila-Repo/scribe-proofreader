# Overview: Document Correction Agent

## Product Intent
Scribe is engineered to serve as a fast, reliable, and thorough editor. It helps creators, developers, and teams produce polished, professional output by eliminating writing friction and errors. By automating the tedious parts of editing, Scribe ensures that documents of any length are coherent, persuasive, and free of embarrassing mistakes.

## Target Audience
- Developers drafting documentation, README files, or release notes.
- Marketing professionals refining copy or blog posts.
- Business executives crafting emails, memos, and proposals.
- Academics and researchers polishing papers.
